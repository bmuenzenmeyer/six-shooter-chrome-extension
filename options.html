Immunity

Pinned tabs sit outside the cylinder: they can't be shot, and they don't take up a chamber. The other two still take up a chamber — they just can't be the one that dies.

If every tab in a full cylinder turns out to be immune, nothing is killed and you go over the limit. Immunity wins over the six.
